Introduction

Lung Cancer is one of the most killer diseases in the world, and the detection of the cancer at early state is a challenge. Analysis and cure of the lung malignancy have been one of the greatest difficulties faced by humans in recent times. Early identification of tumor would facilitate in sparing a huge number of lives around the world. This Project presents lung cancer detection based on chest CT scans using CNN. The main objective of this study is to detect whether the tumor present in a patient's lung is malignant or benign. A web application is built which gives the feasibility of uploading a CT scan image. The model integrated with this app, detects the tumour and the user is shown the predicted result.
